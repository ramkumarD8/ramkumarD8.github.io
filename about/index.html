About Me

I, Ramkumar D, am a doctoral student at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in the Department of Civil Engineering. I work in deep learning modelling to simulate various Wastewater treatment systems technologies. My expertise is applying chaos theory and different hybrid techniques to deep learning modelling to improve the system’s performance. Currently, I am working simultaneously on two projects; one is modelling wastewater treatment systems (funded by the Government of Maharashtra, India) and another exciting study of decoding ancient knowledge of historic temples of India for its relation to temple water infrastructure using deep learning (funded by Indian knowledge systems).
